This is a web version of Kaun Banega Crorepati. Answer the questions correctly to win the game!

It is based on the British game show Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?

KBC first aired in India on 3rd July 2000 on Star Plus. The show was hosted by Bollywood superstar Amitabh Bachchan.

Since then, Amitabh Bachchan has become the face of KBC. Only one season (season 3) was hosted by Shah Rukh Khan.

KBC also highlights various government schemes and initiatives.

The show runs in both Hindi and English
